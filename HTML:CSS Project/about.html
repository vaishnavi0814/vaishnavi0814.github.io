About us

Our Vision

We share a very personal connection with our pets; they are our most faithful of friends. Without question, our dogs are unquestionably irreplaceable members of our family. Show your dog that same unconditional love and affection they show you every day with the very best in dog supplies. Poshpooch.com is your source for everything. We cater to dogs of all sizes and carry all the goodies your dog will love.The right foods, products, and accessories increase their physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Our goal is to offer you the highest quality dog supplies online.

Our Passion

As a socially conscious company we care about all animals, not just those dogs we serve. There is nothing we enjoy more than helping a pet in need of fresh food, a cozy bed, and a place to call home.If you love your dogs as much as we love ours, you’ll fall in love with PoshPooch.com We understand the important relationship between pet owners and their pets. You’ll find everything you want and need to spoil your loveable pet at the lowest prices!
